Common questions

Questions people actually ask.

Don't see your question? Call (647) 243-1018 or email info@armourcoreinc.ca.

Will my windows look tinted?

Only if you want them to. The default safety film is optically clear — you can't tell it's there from inside or outside. We also offer light tints, mirrored solar films, and full privacy frosts where you want them.

Is it bulletproof?

No film makes glass bulletproof. What ArmourCore does — and what's been independently tested — is hold the glass together under repeated impact long enough to defeat a smash-and-grab. For high-threat applications we install heavier laminates and structural attachment systems. We'll spec the right level on your audit.

How is this different from replacing windows with safety glass?

Roughly 80% cheaper, hours instead of weeks, reuses the windows you already paid for, and lab-tested impact performance is comparable on the openings that matter most.

Will my insurance care?

Most commercial brokers in Ontario recognise security film as a hardening upgrade and note it on your policy. Some pass through small premium reductions. We give you the documentation pack to send your broker.

Residential or commercial?

Both, every day. Roughly half-and-half between family homes across Woodbridge and the GTA, and commercial accounts — storefronts, schools, dispensaries, jewellery, and offices.

How long does it take? How messy?

A typical home is finished in a single day. Storefronts are usually done after-hours so you don't lose a day of trading. Drop cloths, dust filtered, vacuumed as we go. We leave every site cleaner than we found it.

What's the warranty?

10-year manufacturer warranty on the film from Madico. Lifetime workmanship guarantee from ArmourCore. Anything ever lifts, bubbles, peels, or fails — we re-do it. No charge.
